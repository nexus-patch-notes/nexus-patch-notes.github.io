Heroes of the Storm Patch Notes

2021-08-20 Live Patch 2.54.3.85894

General

For general changes and/or gameplay updates, check out the official Patch Notes.

Heroes

Deckard

Updates

Base

  • Healing Potion [Q]
    • Medium range flight time reduced from .4375 to .375 seconds.
    • Far range flight time reduced from .6125 to .5 seconds.
    • Heal amount increased from 270 to 285.
  • Horadric Cube [W]
    • Slow duration increased from 1.75 to 2 seconds.

Talents

  • Level 1
    • Scroll of Identify [E]
      • New functionality:
        • Quest: Hitting a Hero with Scroll of Sealing permanently increases its damage by 2.
        • Reward: Hitting 15 Heroes causes Scroll of Sealing to reveal and reduce the Armor of Heroes by 10 for 4 seconds.
        • Reward: Hitting 30 Heroes increases the amount of Armor reduction against Heroes to 15 and its duration to 6 seconds.
  • Level 4
    • Potion of Shielding [Q]
      • Shield increased from 122 to 140.
    • Rejuvenation Potion [Q]
      • Healing increased from 108 over 4 seconds to 114 over 3 seconds.
      • Mana restoration increased from 10 to 15.
  • Level 10
    • Lorenado [R2]
      • Additional functionality: Deals 20 damage to Heroes hit.
  • Level 13
    • Super Healing Potion [Q]
      • Healing bonus reduced from 120 to 100%.
      • Additional functionality:
        • Passive: Potions heal for 25% more against Heroes under 50% health.
    • Potion of Revival [Q]
      • Area healing increased from 50 to 75%.
    • Ancient Blessings [Trait]
      • Cooldown reduced from 60 to 45 seconds.
Developer Comment: Deckard's healing related talents have been underperforming lately, so we're hitting their numbers as well as cutting down some of the travel time on his potions. Scroll of Identify is also getting a blast of ~*spice*~, because why not.

Leoric

Updates

Base

  • Skeletal Swing [Q]
    • Additional functionality: Skeletal Swing deals an additional 75% damage to Minions.
  • Wrath of the Bone King [Trait]
    • Third Basic Attack damage bonus increased from 100% to 125%.

Talents

  • Level 4
    • Kneel, Peasants! [Q]
      • Changed functionality: Skeletal Swing deals an additional 75% damage to Mercenaries and Monsters, and they activate Skeletal Swing's cost and cooldown refund.
Developer Comment: Being the King brings many duties, and being the Skeleton King is no different. Leoric players are often expected to fill a wave clearing role that is dependent on the current Kneel, Peasants! to succeed. We're bringing much of its benefit baseline, and trying something a little weird with Kneel, Peasants! Let us know if you consider it treasonous.

Nazeebo

Updates

Base

  • Corpse Spiders [Q]
    • Cooldown decreased from 9 to 8 seconds.
    • Mana cost reduced from 50 to 40.

Talents

  • Level 1
    • Widowmakers [Q]
      • New functionality:
        • Enemies hit by Corpse Spiders' jar are Slowed by 30% for 1.5 seconds.
        • Reward: After attacking Heroes 100 times, Corpse Spiders deal 30% more damage and last for an additional second.
    • Pandemic [E]
      • Additional functionality: After Hitting 80 Heroes with Plague of Toads, it reduces the damage Heroes deal by 5%, stacking up to 25%.
  • Level 4
    • Blood Ritual [Trait]
      • Additional functionality:
        • Passive: Takedowns grant 5 stacks of Voodoo Ritual.
  • Level 7
    • Spirit of Arachyr [Q]
      • New functionality:
        • Corpse Spiders spawns an additional spider.
        • If Corpse Spiders' jar hits only one target, spawn an additional spider.
  • Level 13
    • Guardian Toads [E]
      • Stack duration increased from 1.5 to 2 seconds.
      • Max duration increased from 4 to 6 seconds.
    • Superstition [Passive]
      • Additional functionality: Heroic Basic Attacks that cancel the Spell Armor effect spawn a Corpse Spider to attack the Hero.
  • Level 16
    • Spider Colony [Q]
      • Additional functionality: Corpse Spider Attacks against Heroes also reduce the cooldown of Corpse Spiders by .125 seconds.
  • Level 20
    • Humongoid [R1]
      • Additional functionality: Gargantuan Stomps' area is increased by 25% and its Slow amount is increased to 50%.
    • Annihilating Spirit [R2]
      • Additional functionality: Heroes hit by Ravenous Spirit have their healing received reduced by 50% for 1 second.
    • Fury of the Storm [Passive]
      • Removed.
    • (New) Bad Medicine [Active]
      • Consume 5 Stacks of Voodoo Ritual to become Unstoppable, gain 20% Movement Speed, and 50 Armor for 2 seconds. 30 second cooldown.
Developer Comment: It's been a hot minute since we touched the Naz man. We like that he's a late-game PvE powerhouse, but we feel there's room for him to have more PvP impact early on. We're adjusting both his Spider and Toad builds to have more of a unique identity and to make them a little more consistent. We're also dumping a pile of jungle spices on his 20s to make them more competitive with the ever-popular Vile Infection.

Zagara

Updates

Base

  • Health reduced from 1575 to 1500.
  • Health regeneration reduced from 3.82 to 3.12.
  • Basic Attack damage reduced from 85 to 83.
  • Baneling Barrage [Q]
    • Mana cost increased from 10 to 15.
    • Cooldown increased from 3 to 4.
    • Damage reduced from 86 to 80.

Talents

  • Level 1
    • Volatile Acid [Q]
      • Removed functionality: No longer increases Baneling Barrage's damage.
  • Level 7
    • Baneling Massacre [Q]
      • Changed functionality: Gain two additional charges of Baneling Barrage, but increase its cooldown by 2 seconds.
  • Level 20
    • Pack Instinct [W]
      • Damage bonus reduced from 25% to 20%.
      • Duration reduced from 3 to 2 seconds.
Developer Comment: In contrast to our usual ethos, Zagara is seeing her talents pay the price for an increase in power in her base ability. While the immediate effect is Zagara has less impactful Baneling talents, we feel that Baneling Barrage is a more interesting ability than from where we started, and we have more room for future changes.
