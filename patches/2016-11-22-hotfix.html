Heroes of the Storm Patch Notes
2016-11-22 Hotfix Patch 1.22.1.48297

General

For general changes and/or gameplay updates, check out the official Patch Notes.

Heroes

Abathur

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed an issue that caused the cooldown for Toxic Nest to be reset whenever Symbiote expired.

Alarak

Bug Fixes

  • Health lost due to activating Last Laugh will no longer be restored when Medivh's Force of Will with the Reabsorption Talent expires.

Medivh

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed an issue that could enable Medivh to cast Abilities while in Raven Form on Warhead Junction.

Samuro

Bug Fixes

  • Mirror Images can no longer launch Nukes on Warhead Junction after learning Illusion Master.

Zeratul

Bug Fixes

  • The Seeker in the Dark Talent will no longer allow Zeratul to Blink to unintended areas of terrain or inside of Structures.
