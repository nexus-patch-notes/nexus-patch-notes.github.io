Heroes of the Storm Patch Notes
2017-10-02 Hotfix Patch 2.28.1.58209

General

For general changes and/or gameplay updates, check out the official Patch Notes.

Heroes

Brightwing

Bug Fixes

  • Phase Shift will no longer be placed on full cooldown when interrupted by Root, Silence, or Polymorph effects.

D.Va

Bug Fixes

  • Will no longer become permanently stuck after colliding with Structures or Terrain after using Boosters.

Diablo

Bug Fixes

  • The Devastating Charge Talent will now correctly apply its bonus damage to enemies stunned by Shadow Charge.

Falstad

Bug Fixes

  • Tailwind's Movement Speed buff will now correctly be placed on cooldown after taking damage.

Gazlowe

Bug Fixes

  • Rock-It! Turrets will now correctly grant allied vision of the area around their placement locations.

Lt. Morales

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed an issue which allowed the Medivac to move toward and pick up allied Heroes on the way to its intended destination.
