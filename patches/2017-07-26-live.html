General

Updates

Abilities

Talents

    • Growing Infestation (E)
      • Added functionality:

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed a typo in the tooltip for Fetid Touch.
  • Stop, Hold Fire, Taunt, and Dance commands can no longer be issued while casting or channeling Lurking Arm.
  • Fixed an issue that could cause FPS to drop to 30 when targeting Lurking Arm.
  • Massive Shove will no longer be cancelled when used on Sgt. Hammer while she is in Siege Mode.
