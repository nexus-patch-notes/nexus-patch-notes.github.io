Heroes

Artanis

Updates

Base

  • Twin Blades [W]
    • Charge range increased from 3 to 3.5.
  • Phase Prism [E]
    • Cast time increased from .0625 seconds to .25 seconds.
    • New functionality:
      • Artanis no longer has to channel while the Phase Prism missile is active.
      • Artanis gains Unstoppable for 1.5 seconds after swapping with an enemy Hero.
  • Purifier Beam [R2]
    • Cooldown reduced from 80 to 70 seconds.
    • Mana cost reduced from 80 to 65.

Talents

  • Level 1
    • Reactive Parry [W]
      • New functionality: Hitting enemy Heroes with Twin Blades or Blade Dash grants a charge of Block for 50 Physical Armor. Stacks up to 2 times.
  • Level 4
    • Shield Battery [Trait]
      • New functionality:
        • Shield Overload's cooldown recharges 125% faster while active.
        • Passive: Reduce Shield Overload's cooldown by 4 seconds.
  • Level 7
    • Follow Through [Passive]
      • Removed.
    • (New) Final Cut [Passive]
      • After using an Ability, your next Basic Attack within 6 seconds deals 40% more damage. When under 25% health, this bonus is increased to 60%.
  • Level 16
    • Blades of a Templar [Passive]
      • Attack speed bonus reduced from 30% to 20%.
  • Level 20
    • Zealot Charge [W]
      • Distance bonus amount reduced from 100% to 75%.
Developer Comment: Artanis has long struggled to get value out of his swaps when facing heavy CC teams and this has a large impact on his viability, especially in higher levels of play. We're giving him a brief window of unstoppability after swapping with Phase Prism, as well as improving the general usability of the ability. We're also giving him a teensy bit more range on his Twin Blades charge. All of these changes combined should help Artanis feel a bit more impactful and safe in team fights.

Morales a little more flexibility in shuttling her allies around earlier in the game.
