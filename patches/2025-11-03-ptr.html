General

Updates

Experience

  • The comeback system has been slightly magnified, particularly at wide level gaps.
    • Experience modifier for Hero kills increased from 0.3 to 0.35 for 2-level gaps.
    • Experience modifier for Hero kills increased from 0.6 to 0.75 for 3-level gaps.
    • Experience modifier for Hero kills increased from 1 to 1.3 for 4-level gaps.
    • Experience modifier for Hero kills increased from 1 to 2 for 5-level gaps.

A Call for Help

  • Forts, Keeps, and Core will no longer grant nearby allied Heroes that take damage 35 Armor for 4 seconds.
  • Basic Attacks from Core, Keeps, Forts, Fallback Towers, and Keep's Towers will now reduce the Armor of enemy Heroes hit.
    • Armor reduction increased from 10 to 20 per hit.
    • Cap increased from 20 to 40 Armor reduction.
  • Damage from the Towers of Doom's Kill Zone now reduces Armor.

Structures

  • Fallback Towers
    • Fallback Towers have been added behind Forts in the longer lanes.
    • Fallback Towers have the same Health and Attack Damage as Keep's Towers.

Gameplay

  • Ability ranges on numerous Heroes have been updated to be more consistent.

Quests

  • Players will now receive a personal-only announcement when an intermediate Quest Reward is unlocked.
