Patch Notes
2017-01-12 Hotfix Patch 1.22.7.49495

General

For general changes and/or gameplay updates, check out the official Patch Notes.

Heroes

Artanis

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed an issue that could prevent Artanis from swapping positions with his target after using Blade Dash and Phase Prism.

Li-Ming

Bug Fixes

  • Basic Attacks that are empowered by the Cannoneer Talent will now correctly trigger the bonus damage granted by the Charged Blast Talent.

Zul'jin

Bug Fixes

  • Kael'thas's Living Bomb will no longer repeatedly explode after being cast on Zul'jin while Taz'dingo is active.
  • Rehgar's Ancestral Healing will no longer fail to heal Zul'jin if it is cast in the same moment that Berserker would reduce his Health to 1.
  • Taz'dingo will now be placed on a reduced cooldown, rather than full cooldown, if it is cast at the exact same moment that he takes fatal damage.
