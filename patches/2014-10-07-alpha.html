General

For general changes and/or gameplay updates, check out the official Patch Notes.

Heroes

Anub'arak

Releases

Anub'arak, Traitor King, has been added to the Nexus and is now available for play! Read on for a brief overview of his Abilities.

  • Trait
    • Scarab Host (D)
      • Spawn a Beetle whenever an Ability is cast.
  • Basic Abilities
    • Impale (Q)
      • Shoot a line of spikes that damages and stuns enemies.
    • Harden Carapace (W)
      • Gain a Shield.
    • Burrow Charge (E)
      • Burrow underground and charge in a direction.
  • Heroic Abilities
    • Locust Swarm (R)
      • Spawn a swarm of Locusts that damage enemies and heal you.
    • Web Blast (R)
      • Wraps an enemy in webbing. Enemies can attack the webbing to break their ally free.

Azmodan

Releases

Azmodan, Lord of Sin, has been added to the Nexus and is now available for play! Read on for a brief overview of his Abilities.

  • Trait
    • General of Hell (D)
      • Azmodan summons a Demon Lieutenant to assist Minions, Mercenaries, and Summons.
  • Basic Abilities
    • Globe of Annihilation (Q)
      • Launch a long-range orb of destruction that deals area damage.
    • Summon Demon Warrior (W)
      • Summon a Demon Warrior.
    • All Shall Burn (E)
      • Channel a death beam on an enemy.
  • Heroic Abilities
    • Demonic Invasion (R)
      • Summons a rain of Demonic Grunts dealing damage on impact.
    • Black Pool (R)
      • Creates a pool that empowers Azmodan and his army.
