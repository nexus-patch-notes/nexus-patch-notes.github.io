Heroes of the Storm Patch Notes

2020-11-04 Live Patch 2.52.2.82624

General

For general changes and/or gameplay updates, check out the official Patch Notes.

Battlegrounds

Warhead Junction

Updates

General

  • Watch Towers
    • A new Watch Tower has been added below the middle lane.
  • Sewage Tunnel
    • Sewage Tunnels have been added to the top and bottom of the battlefield.

Mercenaries

  • Boss Camp
    • The bottom Boss Camp has been removed.
Developer Comment: Since the release of the Battleground, we have been hearing two resounding pieces of feedback: the map is very large and teams find it difficult to engage with each other due to the amount of ground needed to cover while picking up Warheads, and very often the game either ends up being a base-race as each team takes a Junk Swarm Host and tries to push opposite lanes to end fastest. To address this we have added a couple of Sewage Tunnels (think teleporters) to the map, one of which replaces the bottom Junk Swarm Host, and added an additional Watch Tower to allow teams to keep an eye on the bottom tunnel. We feel like these changes have been a breath of fresh air for Warhead Junction and are excited to hear your feedback!

Azmodan

Updates

Talents

  • Talent bonuses related to All Shall Burn will now proc their effects if the target dies while All Shall Burn is channeling on an enemy.
  • This includes effects for Master of Destruction, Cydaea's Kiss, Hell Rift, and Sin's Grasp.
Developer Comment: We saw some feedback that Azmodan's All Shall Burn felt awkward due to not getting various procs if the target dies while he is channeling on them. This was initially intended by us due to concerns of it being too powerful and resulting in some strange moments when playing against it, but we've since come around and agree the lack of usability is hurting these talents and Azmodan as a whole more than it's helping, so we're going to try allowing these procs to occur, even if the target dies.
