General

For general changes and/or gameplay updates, check out the official Patch Notes.

Arthas

Updates

Talents

  • Fixed Arthas's talents so that they appear in the same order as other Heroes [Q, W, E, etc.]
  • Level 4
    • Deathlord [Q]
      • No longer increases the range of Death Coil.
      • Additional functionality: If Death Coil is used on an enemy Hero, its Mana cost is refunded.
Developer Comment: Deathlord has been a fairly awkward talent for a long time, providing Arthas with little output and utility, even when going for a Q build. We're changing it to now give Arthas a fairly potent Poke tool by making it possible for him to infinitely drain the Health of enemy Heroes over time if they stay near him, which should only become more powerful as he gets his other Q talents as the game goes on.

Genji

Updates

Talents

  • Level 4
    • Strike at the Heart [E]
      • Damage dealt reduced from 138 to 134.
      • Damage is now dealt after 1 second instead of over 2 seconds.
  • Level 7
    • Augmented Shield [W]
      • Shield amount increased from 50% to 75% of damage taken.

      • Time before gaining a stack of Dodge reduced from 8 to 7 seconds.

      • Initial damage increased from 135 to 145.

We're also changing a few of his other talents to better compete with their peers.

Junkrat

Updates

Talents

  • Level 4
    • Boom Pow [W]
      • Cooldown reduction increased from 9 to 10 seconds.
  • Level 7
    • Sticky Wicket [E]
      • Silence duration reduced from 2.5 to 2 seconds.
  • Level 10
    • RIP-Tire [R1]
      • Maximum damage reduced from 775 to 720.
      • Minimum damage reduced from 475 to 445.
Developer Comment: Junkrat has been performing very well since his rework. His W build is still not up to par compared to its competitors, but we believe it's very close, so we're slightly nerfing Sticky Wicket while also buffing BOOM POW since it's his lynchpin talent to make the W build come online. We're also nerfing the damage of RIP-Tire, as it is outperforming compared to Rocket Ride, as well as influencing team fights a little much, especially when upgraded at Level 20. We like the powerful, game-breaking feeling that Extra Oomph provides at Level 20 (the dream is that all talents at this stage of the game can feel just as epic), so we're going to hold off on nerfing it until we see how this change pans out.

Lunara

Updates

Base

  • Nature's Toxin [Trait]
    • Damage scaling per level reduced from 5% to 4%.
    • Damage increased from 30 to 36.
Developer Comment: As the information can be a bit hidden, we're re-evaluating where we have scaling on heroes that deviates from the normal 4% per level. While we have some good balance reasons for doing this, Lunara's Nature's Toxin was not one of them. Because of this, we're changing its scaling to match other heroes, which will result in a decent buff in the early-mid game and a very slight nerf to her in the late game. Overall this should be a moderate buff for her for most of the game.

Zagara

Updates

Base

  • Basic Attack damage increased from 76 to 85.

Talents

  • Level 1
    • Volatile Acid [Q]
      • No longer increases Baneling damage against non-Heroic targets.
      • Additional functionality: Increase the damage of Banelings by 20%.
    • Corpse Feeders [E]
      • No longer reduces Roachling damage against non-Heroic sources.
      • Additional functionality: Increase the Health of Roachlings by 30%.
    • Infest [Active]
      • Initial Damage bonus for Ranged Minions increased from 100% to 125%.
  • Level 4
    • Serrated Spines [Passive]
      • Bonus attack damage changed from .3 flat damage to .2% per stack.
  • Level 7
    • Baneling Massacre [Q]
      • No longer increases the damage of Banelings.
      • Additional functionality: Reduce the Charge cooldown of Banelings by .5 seconds.
  • Level 10
    • Devouring Maw [R1]
      • Mana cost reduced from 100 to 70.
      • Damage increased from 88 to 94.
  • Level 16
    • Brood Expansion [W]
      • Removed.
    • Corrosive Saliva [W]
      • Damage reduced from 2.5% to 1.75% maximum Health.
      • Additional functionality: Now also causes Roachlings to deal additional damage equal to 1.75% maximum Health to enemy Heroes.
    • Mutalisk [W]
      • Additional functionality: Reduce the cooldown of Hunter Killer by 4 seconds.
    • New Talent: Jagged Barbs [Trait]
      • When on Creep, Zagara's attack range is increased by an additional 10% and her Basic Attack damage is increased by 30%.
Developer Comment: We're changing around quite a bit about Zagara, with a few goals in mind. Firstly, we believe we went a little far in reducing her power in the solo-lane, so we're increasing her Basic Attack damage, which will help her throughout the game in a way that enemies should always be able to react to and punish her accordingly if she misplays. We're also changing around her Level 1 talents to be more universally usable outside of PvE scenarios. For Zagara players who want to specialize in Sieging enemy bases, Infest will be the go-to talent on the tier, whereas the other talents will be more generally useful. Lastly, we're significantly buffing her late-game power, but making her Level 16 talents fairly impactful, which should help her scale into the late game as a competent damage dealer during team fights.
